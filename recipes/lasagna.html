How to make the best lasagna ever

Ingredients

  • Lasagne sheets
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Minced beef
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • White sauce

Method

  1. Make the bolognese
  2. Get the white sauce out the jar
  3. Put the bolognese in a dish
  4. Layer white sauce on top
  5. Do this for another layer
  6. Put cheese on top
  7. Bake
